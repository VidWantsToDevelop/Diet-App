If you would like to choose one of the given plans as your current, you will be receiving navigations upon your chosen plan on your profile page. Moreover, here you can find out more information about certain diet plans, some advices as well as pros and cons.

The Paleo Diet

The paleo diet claims that you should eat the same foods that your hunter-gatherer ancestors ate before agriculture developed.

Weight loss; Reducing risk factors for heart disease

Eliminates healthy grains

The vegan diet

The vegan diet restricts all animal products for ethical, environmental, or health reasons.

Weight loss; Reducing risk of Alzheimer's disease

Because vegan diets eliminate animal foods completely, they may be low in several nutrients.

Low-Carb Diet

There are several types of low-carb diets, but all involve limiting carb intake to 20–150 grams per day. The primary aim of the diet is to force your body to use more fats for fuel instead of using carbs as a main source of energy.

Weight loss; Reduce your appetite

Danger of "bad" LDL cholesterol

The Dukan Diet

The Dukan diet is a high-protein, low-carb weight loss diet split into four phases — two weight loss phases and two maintenance phases.

Weight loss; Higher metabolic rate

Fast weight loss achieved by severe calorie restriction tends to cause significant muscle loss.

The Ultra-Low-Fat Diet

The paleo diet claims that you should eat the same foods that your hunter-gatherer ancestors ate before agriculture developed.

Reducing factors of heart disease, improvement in type 2 diabetes

The fat restriction may cause long-term problems, these include helping build cell membranes.

Intermittent Fasting

Rather than restricting the foods you eat, it controls when you eat them. Thus, it can be seen as more of an eating pattern than a diet.

Increased levels of human growth hormone (HGH), improved insulin sensitivity, improved cellular repair, and altered gene expressions

It does not suit everyone.
